Biography

Moyosore Sulola was on May 13, 1996, in Ogun State, Nigeria to Oluwatisegun and Oluwagbemiga Sulola. He has five older siblings. Moyosore attended Yemi International College and Emmanuel Comprehensive College before going to Tertiary education at the Federal Polytechnic Ilaro, Ogun State.

Following his desires and dreams, he studied Computer Science at the Federal Polytechnic Ilaro. In 2018, during his final year, he was privileged to be selected as the President of the Christian Union Cathedral on Campus, for both students and lecturers.

In 2019, Moyosore was posted for National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) to Okopedi, Ibono Ibom, Akwa Ibom State, where he served as a Geography and English Teacher. During his service year, he was involved in Community Service that entails reaching out to the children in the community with Educational materials and also hosting sensitization sessions for them on Covid-19 prevention. After the Covid-19 lock-down in 2020, he moved to Uyo, which is the State's capital, and has been there till now.

Moyosore started working as a Video Editor and Graphics designer in a remote office studio in Uyo. He also start learning and working as a Broadcast Engineer for about, then he was moved to the System Administration department. This continue till he resigned his job to pursue his Tech dream.


Joining AltSchool Africa

In 2022, Moyosore came across a post regarding AltSchool Africa, however, he was currently handling a job that 9am to 7pm, and as such didn't have the luxury of time to the training. He also didn't have the required gadgets for the training. So he started saving up to first of all get the required gadgets. Once this was done in 2024, Moyosore made up his mind to go all out for his Tech dreams. The AltSchool AFrica 2024 cohort was already closed by this time, so he applied for the Baraka 2025 cohort.

With much resilience and dedication, Moyosore aced the entrance exams, having 90% of 100%. Which this score, he applied for the Ayoba Developer Scholarship Fund Program, and he was selected as one of the qualifiers.

With all the Testimonies that abound both on X and LinkedIn, being admitted in AltSchool Africa and being awarded a fully funded Scholarship is a dream come through for Moyosore to pursue his career change path. Moyosore looks forward to the training and opportunities that awaits him at AltSchool Africa.


Goals for the School of Engineering Program

With the trend of technology redefining the future, Moyosore intends to flow with this stream by building the relevant skills with AltSchool Africa and playing a part in reshaping the future and providing solution to real-world problems using technology.
